Our mission

It all started in Vermont in 1997. Our passion for protecting the environment led us to our mission:
to use the power of consumer choice to change the way power is made.
Today, as the longest-serving renewable energy retailer, we remain committed to sustainability
every step of the way. By offering only products with an environmental benefit and operating with a
zero-carbon footprint, we’re living our promise to the planet, inside and out.
